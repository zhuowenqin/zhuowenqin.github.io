I am a PhD student in the Department of Political Economy at King's College London, supervised by Yonatan Berman, Bouke Klein Teeselink, and Theo Serlin. My research interests lie in social norms, public economics, housing inequality, and urban economics.
Before King's, I received a Master of Management in Public Policy from Fudan University and a B.A. in German from Nanjing University. I also spent a semester at Sciences Po Paris and at the University of Tübingen.
